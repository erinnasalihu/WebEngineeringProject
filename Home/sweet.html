Classic Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions:

Cream butter and sugar together. Add egg and vanilla. Mix in flour until a dough forms. Roll, cut, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8–10 minutes.

Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter (for cooking)

Instructions:

Mix flour, sugar, milk, and egg until smooth. Cook on a greased skillet until bubbles form, then flip. Serve with syrup or jam.

Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking powder (optional for fluffiness)

Instructions:

Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs one at a time. Alternate adding flour and milk, mixing until smooth. Pour into cupcake liners and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 18–20 minutes.

Sweet Crepes

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter (for cooking)

Instructions:

Whisk flour, sugar, milk, and eggs into a smooth batter. Heat a non-stick pan, melt butter, and pour a thin layer of batter. Cook until edges lift, then flip. Fill with fruits, jam, or Nutella.

Simple Shortbread Cookies

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Instructions:

Mix flour, sugar, and butter until a dough forms. Roll and cut into shapes or press into a baking tray. Bake at 325°F (165°C) for 20–25 minutes until golden.
