Garlic Bread Toast

Ingredients:

  • Bread (any kind)
  • Butter
  • Garlic (minced or powdered)
  • Salt
  • Herbs (optional, like parsley or oregano)

Instructions:

Mix softened butter with garlic and salt. Spread on bread slices and toast in a pan or oven until golden. Sprinkle with herbs for extra flavor.
