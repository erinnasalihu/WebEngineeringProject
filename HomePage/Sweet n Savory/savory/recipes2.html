Veggie Fritters

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Grated vegetables (like zucchini, carrot, or potato)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil (for frying)

Instructions:

Mix grated vegetables with flour, an egg, salt, and pepper to form a batter. Heat oil in a skillet and drop spoonfuls of the mixture into the pan. Fry until golden on both sides.
