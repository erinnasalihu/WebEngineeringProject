Cheese Omelette

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cheese (grated)
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

Whisk eggs with a splash of milk, salt, and pepper. Melt butter in a pan and pour in the egg mixture. Add grated cheese before folding the omelette in half.

Veggie Fritters

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Grated vegetables (like zucchini, carrot, or potato)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil (for frying)

Instructions:

Mix grated vegetables with flour, an egg, salt, and pepper to form a batter. Heat oil in a skillet and drop spoonfuls of the mixture into the pan. Fry until golden on both sides.

Garlic Bread Toast

Ingredients:

  • Bread (any kind)
  • Butter
  • Garlic (minced or powdered)
  • Salt
  • Herbs (optional, like parsley or oregano)

Instructions:

Mix softened butter with garlic and salt. Spread on bread slices and toast in a pan or oven until golden. Sprinkle with herbs for extra flavor.

Simple Pasta Sauce with Egg

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic (optional)

Instructions:

Boil pasta (you can use basic flour-based pasta or make fresh dough). Sauté garlic in butter, scramble an egg into the pan, and mix with milk or pasta water. Combine with cooked pasta and season with salt and pepper.

Savory Crepes

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter (for cooking)
  • Cheese or vegetables (for filling)

Instructions:

Make a batter with flour, milk, eggs, and a pinch of salt. Cook thin crepes in a buttered skillet. Fill with cheese, sautéed vegetables, or ham before folding and serving.
